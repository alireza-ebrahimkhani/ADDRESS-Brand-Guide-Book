Logo

Concept

Transparent, effortless, reliable and efficient; Our brand is a reflection of who we are and what we do. We are all about recreating the experience of renting a home to make it convenient and delightful.
Our visual identity is a simple, clear and direct representation of “having a roof over your head”. Inspired by the general symbol of a house, the letter ”آ (Persian A)” is formed, which acts as an independent sign and as a part of the logotype.
